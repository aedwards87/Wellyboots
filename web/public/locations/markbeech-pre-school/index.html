Markbeech

Markbeech Wellyboots is the seventh and last of the wellyboots chain and is located within Markbeech Village Hall. We have two play spaces with utilities and kitchen facilities. Wellyboots Pre-School children can attend from 9-3pm Monday to Thursday.

Wellyboots Pre-School at Markbeech is a newly established setting. Our staff team is highly experienced and passionate, who work together to maintain a high-quality, nurturing environment, which balances a routine that encourages our children to progress to their full potential.

Daily, the children attend age-appropriate and or adult-initiated experiences and activities delivering our Early Years Foundation curriculum.

Markbeech Wellyboots Pre-School is a feeder Pre-Shcool to Hever School.

Meet our staff

All our staff have significant experience and hold relevant qualifications required for working with children and young people, and comply with EYFS requirements for registered childcare.

All the Staff are expected to undertake professional development training regularly and are checked through the Disclosure and barring service (D.B.S).

First aid, food hygiene and safe guarding children courses are updated as required.
